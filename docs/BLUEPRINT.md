# REACTOR-X

> Not just a portfolio. An interactive cinematic experience.

---

# Vision

REACTOR-X is an interactive developer portfolio designed as a futuristic AI research facility.

Visitors do not browse pages.

They explore a world.

The application combines modern frontend engineering, 3D graphics, cinematic animations, and storytelling into one experience.
